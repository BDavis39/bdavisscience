Current Research

Through an analysis of an evolving boundary layer estimated from
observations, my research seeks to provide insights into flux
partitioning at the surface, specifically for the purpose of
understanding the complex interactions between the atmosphere
and Earth's surface at different times and locations. As a first step,
testing data produced from a single column model is being used to
refine the ABL estimation method for a more accurate prediction of
boundary layer behavior.
